Age is the Biggest Factor

A car's value depreciates most sharply in its first 3-5 years. After that, the price drop becomes more gradual.

Diesel vs. Petrol

Diesel cars, especially SUVs, tend to hold their value slightly better than petrol models due to perceived durability and mileage.

Dealer vs. Individual

Cars sold by dealers are typically listed at a higher price, but often come with a warranty. Individual sellers offer lower prices but more risk.
